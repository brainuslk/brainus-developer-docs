Learn how to upgrade your plan, what happens during the upgrade, and how billing works.

## How to Upgrade

### Via Dashboard

1. Go to **[Dashboard → Settings → Billing](https://developers.brainus.lk/dashboard/settings)**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Select your desired plan (Pro or Enterprise)
4. Complete payment with credit card or invoice
5. New limits apply immediately

## What Happens When You Upgrade

### Immediate Effects

**New rate limits** apply instantly
**Additional API keys** available immediately
**Advanced features** enabled right away
**Existing API keys** continue to work

### Billing

- **Prorated charges** for mid-cycle upgrades
- **No downtime** during the upgrade
- **Keep all your data** and settings
- **Invoice sent** to your email

<Callout type="info">
  When you upgrade, you're only charged the prorated difference for the
  remaining billing period.
</Callout>

## Downgrading

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select a lower plan
4. Confirm the change

### What to Know

- ⚠️ **Lower limits** apply immediately
- ⚠️ **Excess API keys** will be disabled
- ⚠️ **Advanced features** may be removed
- **Credit applied** to next billing cycle

<Callout type="warn">
  If you've exceeded the new plan's quota when downgrading, API requests will be
  rate-limited until the next billing period.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

### What happens to my API keys?

All your existing API keys remain valid and automatically inherit the new plan's rate limits.

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Contact [developers@brainus.lk](mailto:developers@brainus.lk) for refunds.

### Can I switch between monthly and yearly billing?

Yes, you can switch billing periods at any time. The change applies at your next billing date.

### What payment methods do you accept?

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfers (Enterprise only)
- Invoicing (Enterprise only)

### Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for any plan. You only pay the monthly or yearly subscription price.

### Can I pause my subscription?

Free plans don't require payment. Paid plans can be downgraded to Free at any time, which effectively pauses your subscription.
